Our Philosophy

Nature as the Art

At Fusion Garden Inc, we believe that a garden is the most alive form of design — it grows, changes with the seasons, and becomes more beautiful with time. We don't just plant; we compose.


Founded by landscape architect Clara Voss in 2009, our studio fuses European garden traditions with modern ecological thinking and a passionate love for plants. Every project we take on is treated as a unique canvas.

Our Story

Rooted in
Passion

Fusion Garden Inc was founded in 2009 by Clara Voss — a trained landscape architect who studied in Berlin and worked on some of Europe's most celebrated public gardens before returning home with a vision: to bring that standard of design thinking to residential and commercial gardens in North America.


The "fusion" in our name reflects our belief that the best gardens fuse styles — the formal precision of European design with the wildness of native ecology, the ancient tradition of the kitchen garden with the modernist outdoor room. Our 22-person team brings together horticulturalists, architects, irrigation engineers and garden maintenance specialists.


In 15 years we've created over 840 gardens — from window box herb collections to 40-acre country estates. We approach every single one with the same curiosity and care.
